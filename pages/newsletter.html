Community Impact — April 2026

Farmers from Nyanga harvested over a ton of spinach this month, with each contributing member receiving their proportional payout within 48 hours of sale.

New irrigation channels funded by last quarter's equipment pool are already showing results, with water usage down 30% across participating plots.

We welcomed 14 new farming families this April, bringing the total community membership to 312 households across 14 townships.

Seed sharing initiative launched — members can now list surplus seeds through the platform free of charge.

This Month in Numbers

Total produce sold: 4,820 kg across all communities.

Average payout per farmer: R3,750 — up 18% from March.

Equipment pool purchases: 3 drip irrigation systems and 12 hand tool sets.

New members joined: 14 households 3 from Khayelitsha, 4 from Mitchells Plain, 4 from Gugulethu, 2 from Bonteheuwel and 1 from Langa.

Spotlight: Equipment Sharing

This month we piloted a tool-sharing rotation between three neighbouring plots. Farmers reported saving an average of R320 each by borrowing instead of buying.

The rotation system is now being rolled out to any community that would like to join. Contact us to learn more.

Next month's bulk order closes on the 20th — submit your equipment requests before then to qualify for the discount.
