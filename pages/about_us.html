About Us

Our Mission
“

To empower township farmers by giving every farmer a fair, proportional share of what they grow and the tools they need to grow more. Through this we can enable food and job secuirity for our communities and create a more just and sustainable food system for all.

”
Our Story

How Roots Began

Roots began as a small project with 3 households in Makhaza, Kayeltsha in early 2020. Our farmers had extra crops that they would sell to their neighbours but they still had excess and needed another way to also sell.

We wanted to find a way to support local farmers and create a more equitable food system, so we started by connecting with a few farmers we knew and helping them sell their produce directly to buyers in the city and other areas.

Today, we are continuously expanding to more communities and farmers, whilst we grow our opperations and are able to provide more support and tools to better serve our farmers and buyers. We are proud of how far we've come and are excited for the future as we continue to grow together with our communities.

How It Works

1. Sign up

Get in touch with us and apply to learn if you can join the network. We also provide some resources to help if work still has to be done.

2. Contribute Your Harvest

Farmers contribute their harvest by delivery to our distribution center or through a pickup service we provide at a small cost.

3. Earn Your Share

Farmers receive payment based on the quantity and quality of their produce. The "proportional share" is calculated by considering the total produce sold and the individual contribution of each farmer. Small farms won't be chraged for their first 6 months of selling.

Our Values

Fairness

We are looking to uplift these communities by ensuring fair compensation, support and transparent practices for all our farmers.

Community First

We prioritize the needs and voices of our community members in all our decisions, ensuring that they are at the center of our efforts and that we reinvest in local infrastructure.

Sustainability

We are committed to promoting sustainable farming practices that protect our environment and ensure long-term productivity. This includes focusing on soil health, efficient water use, planting seasonal crops, and encouraging organic practices.

Transparency

Farmers will get detailed reports every month showing exactly what was sold, at what price, where the sales went and what their share will be.
